Entry 2:

Bruno & Izzy

13th st & 4th ave

Was on the way to the UC with a friend when Bruno came running over to us! a 4 month-old boy who can’t wait to mingle and have so much fun.
Eagerly ran in circles and up and down, in and out of my lap with Izzy!

“... he just wants to play... he’s gonna get bigger too, he’s just 4 months old,”...
“aww stop, he’s going to be such a big boy!”

Izzy couldn’t wait to meet Bruno, was just as eager to play and receive pets from my friend and I. 8 months and enough energy to power two apartment buildings in the city.



Freddie

Stuy Park Residence

Freddie!

Freddie came by my building for a visit and we collectively spent a good half hour catching up and passing him around like we were playing pass-the-parcel.

“he’s so tiny!”,
“freDDIEEeee!”
“is he going to get any bigger?”

He was so sleepy, but don’t let that fool you. We were told that he bullies his older sister, Honey Dew by stealing her food and locking her in her crate when they’re alone. Tsk tsk....
